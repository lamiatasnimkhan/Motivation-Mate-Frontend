Pomodoro Timer

Work
Break

The Pomodoro Technique turns time into a game! Work in focused 25-minute bursts called "Pomodoros," then reward yourself with a 5-minute break. After four Pomodoros, take a longer break to recharge. It's all about working smarter, not harder, and keeping your productivity on point while still making time to relax!
